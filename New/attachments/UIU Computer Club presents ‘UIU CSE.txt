UIU Computer Club presents ‘UIU CSE WEEK’

UIU Computer Club (UIUCCL), one of the leading clubs of Directorate of Career Counseling and Student Affairs (DCCSA) of United International University (UIU), is going to present ‘UIU CSE Week’. It is an intra-week-long tech competition designed to unlock the boundless potential opportunities for the students of CSE of UIU.  Throughout the week, participants would be introduced to hands-on workshops that will develop their hard and soft skills through a test of their knowledge in their respective fields.
