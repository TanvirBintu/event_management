About us

United International University (UIU) is a private university located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Established in 2003, UIU has quickly gained a reputation for its commitment to quality education and innovative teaching methodologies. The university offers a wide range of academic programs, including undergraduate and postgraduate degrees, in various disciplines such as Business Administration, Engineering, Computer Science, and Liberal Arts. UIU is known for its modern campus facilities and well-equipped classrooms that facilitate a conducive learning environment for students. The university places a strong emphasis on research and practical application of knowledge, encouraging students to engage in projects and activities that foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills. One of UIU's notable features is its diverse and highly qualified faculty members who come from both academia and industry backgrounds. This blend of expertise helps bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications. The university also places significance on extracurricular activities, offering students opportunities to participate in clubs, sports, and cultural events, fostering a holistic development approach. UIU's commitment to producing well-rounded graduates with a global perspective is reflected in its partnerships with international institutions, which provide avenues for student and faculty exchange programs. Over the years, United International University has garnered recognition for its academic excellence, research contributions, and community engagement.
